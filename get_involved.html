Volunteer

Our volunteers are amazing people who give their time to support our programs and events in the Philippines

Who can volunteer?

Our volunteers range from 18 to 85 years old. They come from all different backgrounds and bring diverse skill sets to
our organisation. We value the support of every single one of our volunteers.

We would love it that you become a Hope for Children volunteer!



How your time will help children

Volunteering is a great way to meet new people and become part of a community of committed supporters. It also gives
you the opportunity to improve your skills and career prospects, and of course you'll be playing a vital role in helping us
to have a Hope for children in their everyday lives.


The benefits of volunteering for you

You're not only help kids when you volunteer for Hope for Children; there are also big benefits for you, too:

You'll learn new skills while using your existing ones.
You'll meet new people and make wonderful new friends.
You'll find out more about the issues affecting vulnerable communities both here in the Philippines and overseas.
You'll be going behind the scenes at one of the Philippines' largest aid and development agencies.


Current volunteer opportunities

You can apply for a specific role or register your interest in volunteering if we don't have the right opportunity for you.
If there is something available, we will get in touch. Otherwise we will keep you on file and contact you if something
suitable becomes available.
